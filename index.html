From Classrooms to Laboratories

It started as a middle school dream: eating in class without that "granola bar explosion" sound. After years of development, we realized we couldn't do it alone. We needed the best in the world.

LUMIA LABS Partnership

We collaborated with Lumia Labs, the world leaders in high-fidelity acoustics, to develop our Piezoelectric Silk. Together, we engineered a fabric that actively cancels the high-frequency crinkle of snack wrappers.

ECOLIFE Partnership

ECOLIFE specializes in manufacturing premium, high-quality eco-friendly materials. They source our sustainable Swiss fibers, ensuring your stealth doesn't come at a cost to the environment.

What started as a funny memory is now a reality. Built in Switzerland, designed with science, and made for the hungry.
