From School Smugglers to Swiss Engineering

Back in middle school, my two best friends and I were self-proclaimed snack smugglers. Our mission was simple: eat in class without getting caught. One time I tried to open a granola bar as quietly as possible, but it still sounded like an explosion. The class went silent, and the teacher just gave me that disappointed look.

Years later, we realized that problem was still around. Bags were full, pockets were tiny, and crinkly wrappers were the enemy. That’s when I came up with Snack-Hood.

What started as a funny middle school memory turned into real Swiss-engineered textile innovation. Now, you can finally enjoy your snacks in peace.
