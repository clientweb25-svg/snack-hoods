Our Mission

Back in middle school, we were self-proclaimed snack smugglers. Our mission was simple: eat in class without getting caught. One explosion of a granola bar wrapper later, we knew we needed a better way.

We partnered with Lumia Labs 🔊 to develop high-tech noise-canceling fabric and ECOLIFE 🌿 to manufacture premium, eco-friendly Swiss materials. What started as a detention-room dream is now the ultimate gear for gamers and students alike.
