From Smugglers to Founders

In middle school, our mission was simple: eat in class without getting caught. One granola bar explosion later, the whole room went silent. We failed. But we learned.

Years later, we realized the hassle remained. Bags were bulky and wrappers were loud. We turned that detention-room dream into Snack-Hood: an insulating hoodie with a binder-like compartment that keeps food fresh and easy to carry without the bulk.
