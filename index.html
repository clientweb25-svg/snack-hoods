Our Story

We eventually came up with the Snack-Hoods! A revolutionary hoodie using a removable binder contraption, fitted with our advanced patented material designed to eliminate sound leakage while ensuring environmental efforts via collaboration with Lumia Labs and ECO-LIFE.
